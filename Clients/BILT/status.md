## About BILT

**What they do:** Intelligent instructions for augmented work. BILT provides interactive 3D instructions that help users assemble, install, maintain, and repair products and equipment.

**Go-to-Market Segments:**

| Segment | Use Case | Audience |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Consumer Durables** | Home assembly instructions | Consumers via brands & retailers |
| **Government** | Installation, maintenance, repair of complex machinery, vehicles, systems; technician training | Federal, state, defense |
| **Commercial Enterprises** | Installation, maintenance, repair of industrial equipment; technician training | Field service, manufacturing, facilities |
