## Executive Summary

Curinos operates in the financial services data and analytics market, competing with established players ranging from large financial data providers to specialized fintech analytics companies. The competitive landscape is characterized by:

- **Broad platforms** (S&P Global, Moody's) with extensive data but less specialization
- **Specialized competitors** (ProSight, Darling Consulting) with deep financial institution focus
- **Emerging analytics players** (Earnix, KlariVis) with modern technology stacks
- **Consulting firms** (McKinsey) offering strategic insights alongside data

**Curinos' Positioning:** Exclusive focus on financial institutions, combined data+analytics+platforms approach, formed from merger of respected legacy brands (Novantas + FBX).

## Major Competitors

### 1. S&P Global Market Intelligence

**Company Overview:**
- Major financial data and analytics provider
- Part of S&P Global family
- Extensive datasets across financial services

**Products/Solutions:**
- Banking Essentials
- Depository rates data from RateWatch
- SNL sector-specific metrics
- Demographics data from Claritas
- Commercial properties data from CoreLogic
- UCC Filings from Experian

**Strengths:**
- Massive data assets and breadth
- Brand recognition and reputation
- Financial institution trust
- Integrated data from multiple sources
- Strong analyst relations and market intelligence

**Weaknesses:**
- Broad focus (not FI-exclusive)
- May lack depth in deposit/pricing optimization
- Complex platform with learning curve
- Can be expensive

**Where We Compete:**
- Deposit rankings and benchmarking
- Community bank analytics
- Pricing data and competitive intelligence

**Differentiation Opportunities:**
- Our exclusive FI focus vs. their broad market approach
- Purpose-built optimization tools vs. general analytics
- Depth in deposits/pricing vs. breadth across all metrics

**Sources:**
- Website: https://www.spglobal.com/marketintelligence/en/campaigns/us-banks

### 2. ProSight Financial Association (formerly BAI/RMA)

- Financial institution trade association research arm
- Deep relationships with top retail banks
- Focus on consumer banking benchmarking

**Products/Solutions:**
- Consumer Deposits Benchmarking
- Retail Banking Benchmarking

**Strengths:**

- Consumer banking analytics

**Differentiation Opportunities:**
- Our optimization tools vs. their benchmarking focus
- Technology platform vs. research reports
- Real-time insights vs. periodic benchmarking cycles

## Competitive Positioning Framework

### Where Curinos Wins

**Exclusive Financial Institution Focus:**
- Unlike S&P Global or Moody's, Curinos ONLY serves FIs - this specialization matters

**Data + Analytics + Platform + Advisory:**
- Comprehensive approach vs. point solutions
- Not just data (S&P), not just consulting (McKinsey), not just platform (Earnix)

**Breadth Across Balance Sheet:**
- Deposits (vs. Darling), Commercial (vs. ProSight), Digital, Profitability
- Cross-product insights competitors can't match

**Heritage and Trust:**
- Novantas legacy in deposit pricing
- FBX legacy in digital banking
- 800+ FI clients, 42 of top 50 mortgage lenders, Big 6 Canadian banks

**Data Scale:**
- 3,600+ financial institutions in pricing data
- Proprietary behavioral data and price sensitivity models

### Where We May Face Challenges

**Against S&P Global:**
- Brand recognition and breadth
- Need to prove value of specialization

**Against ProSight:**
- Association model and peer trust
- Need to show platform superiority

**Against Earnix:**
- Modern tech and UI/UX
- Need to prove data depth matters more than tech

**Against McKinsey:**
- Strategic consulting cachet
- Need to show platform delivers ongoing value vs. one-time project
