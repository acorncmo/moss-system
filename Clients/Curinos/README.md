# Curinos Client Folder - User Guide

Welcome to your Curinos client management system. This folder structure has been designed to support your fractional CMO workflow with time-based organization and comprehensive templates.

---

## Folder Structure Overview

```
Curinos/
├── 01-Strategy/              # Strategic planning documents
├── 02-Meetings/             # Meeting notes organized by type
├── 03-Reports/              # Performance reports (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
├── 04-Templates/            # Reusable templates for all documents
├── 05-Projects/             # Individual project folders
├── 06-Resources/            # Brand assets and competitive intelligence
└── 07-Working/              # Current work in progress
```

---

## How to Use This System

### Getting Started (Before Monday)

1. **Read First:** `First-Week-Onboarding.md`
   - Your day-by-day guide for the first week
   - Checklist of people to meet, access to request, and tasks to complete

2. **Set Up Your Week:** `07-Working/Weekly-Planner-Template.md`
   - Plan your first week using this template
   - Save as `07-Working/Current-Week/Week-of-[DATE].md`

3. **Review Strategy Templates:** `01-Strategy/`
   - Familiarize yourself with the 90-Day Plan framework
   - You'll use this after your first week of discovery

---

## Daily Workflow

### Every Monday Morning
1. Copy `Weekly-Planner-Template.md` to `07-Working/Current-Week/`
2. Review last week's status report
3. Set your top 3 priorities for the week
4. Review action items tracker

### Before Each Meeting
1. Copy appropriate template from `04-Templates/`
2. Save to relevant folder in `02-Meetings/`
3. Do any required prep work

### After Each Meeting
1. Complete meeting notes
2. Extract action items to `Action-Items-Tracker.md`
3. Follow up within 24 hours

### Every Friday
1. Complete weekly status report (use template in `03-Reports/Weekly/`)
2. Update action items tracker
3. Review and close out weekly planner
4. Plan next week

## Key Documents & When to Use Them

### Strategy Documents (`01-Strategy/`)

**90-Day-Plan.md**
- **When:** After your first 2 weeks of discovery
- **Purpose:** Set strategic direction for your first 90 days
- **Update:** Review weekly, update monthly

**Marketing-Strategy-Framework.md**
- **When:** After 90 days, or when developing annual strategy
- **Purpose:** Comprehensive marketing strategy documentation
- **Update:** Quarterly review, annual refresh

**OKR-Goals-Tracker.md**
- **When:** Beginning of each quarter
- **Purpose:** Track objectives and key results
- **Update:** Weekly check-ins, monthly reviews

### Reporting Templates (`03-Reports/`)

**Weekly Status Report**
- **Frequency:** Every Friday
- **Location:** `03-Reports/Weekly/[DATE]-Weekly-Status.md`

**Monthly Performance Report**
- **Frequency:** First week of each month
- **Location:** `03-Reports/Monthly/[MONTH]-Performance-Report.md`

- **Frequency:** Update weekly, review monthly

## Productivity Best Practices

### Time-Based Organization

**Weekly Focus**
- Monday: Plan and prioritize
- Tuesday-Thursday: Execute and meet
- Friday: Report and prep for next week

**Monthly Rhythm**
- Week 1: Report on previous month, plan current month
- Week 2-3: Execute initiatives
- Week 4: Prepare for next month, monthly review

**Quarterly Cadence**
- Weeks 1-4: Discovery and planning (new quarters)
- Weeks 5-10: Execute and optimize
- Weeks 11-12: Review and prepare next quarter

---

### Action Items Management

1. **Single Source of Truth**
   - All action items go in `Action-Items-Tracker.md`
   - Reference this daily

2. **Priority System**
   - High: Must be done this week
   - Medium: Should be done this week
   - Low: Nice to do or next week

3. **Daily Review**
   - Check tracker every morning
   - Update status as you complete tasks
   - Move overdue items to current week's planner

---

### Meeting Efficiency

**Before:**
- Always use a template
- Send agenda 24 hours in advance
- Do your homework

**During:**
- Take notes in the template
- Capture action items in real-time
- Confirm next steps before ending

**After:**
- Send notes within 24 hours
- Add action items to tracker
- Follow up on commitments

## Tips for Success

1. **Start Simple**
   - Don't try to use every template immediately
   - Begin with Weekly Planner, Action Items Tracker, and Meeting Templates
   - Add others as you establish rhythm

2. **Establish Routine**
   - Same time every week for planning and reporting
   - Consistent meeting schedules
   - Regular review cycles

3. **Stay Organized**
   - File meeting notes promptly
   - Keep action items updated
   - Archive completed work

4. **Be Flexible**
   - Adjust templates to fit Curinos' culture
   - Skip sections that don't apply
   - Add sections you need

5. **Communicate Consistently**
   - Use templates to maintain quality
   - Regular status updates build trust
   - Document everything for continuity
