## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Limited time (5 months) to create lasting change
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on highest-impact activities
- Document everything for sustainability
- Train internal teams for ownership
- Build repeatable processes early

### Risk: Alignment challenges across Product, Sales, Marketing
**Mitigation:**
- Weekly touchpoints with key stakeholders
- Create shared frameworks and language
- Involve stakeholders in creation, not just review
- Celebrate wins and share credit

### Risk: ABM program requires ongoing resources
**Mitigation:**
- Build scalable, repeatable plays
- Train Demand Gen team on ABM execution
- Use technology to automate where possible
- Document playbook for future execution

### Risk: Product launches may shift timelines
**Mitigation:**
- Build GTM templates for flexibility
- Stay close to Product roadmap changes
- Have backup activities if launches delay
- Focus on evergreen enablement alongside launches
