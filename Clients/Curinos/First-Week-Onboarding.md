## About Curinos - Quick Context

**What They Do:** Leading provider of data, technologies, and insights for financial institutions (banks, credit unions). Formed in 2021 from merger of Novantas and Informa's FBX.

**Key Products:**
- Retail Deposit Optimizer (deposit analytics & optimization)
- Commercial Analyzer (commercial deposits & treasury management)
- Digital Banking Analyzer
- Profitability Optimizer
- Pricing Data (3,600+ FI data sources)

**Customers:** 800+ credit unions and community banks, 42 of top 50 mortgage lenders, Canada's Big Six banks

**2026 Market Context:** First "AI-Native" year in banking - compressed margins, M&A activity, fintech competition, battle for primary relationships

**Your Role:**
- Own: Product Marketing and Account-Based Marketing (ABM)
- Collaborate: Commercial team (demand gen, communications)
- Align: Product and Sales executives
